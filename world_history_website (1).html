Prehistoric Times

Early human history before written records, including the Paleolithic, Mesolithic, and Neolithic eras.

Ancient Civilizations

Civilizations such as Sumer, Egypt, the Indus Valley, Ancient China, and Mesoamerican cultures.

Classical Antiquity

Includes Ancient Greece, Rome, Persia, and the Maurya and Gupta Empires in India.

Late Antiquity

The transformation of the Roman world, the spread of Christianity, and the rise of new empires.

Medieval Era

Feudal societies, the Crusades, Mongol invasions, and the Islamic Golden Age.

Renaissance and Reformation

Renewed interest in classical knowledge, humanism, and the religious reform movements in Europe.

Enlightenment and Revolutions

The Age of Reason, American and French Revolutions, and early human rights discourse.

Industrial Age

The rise of mechanization, urbanization, and global empires in the 18th and 19th centuries.

Modern History

World Wars, decolonization, and the Cold War shaping the 20th century.

Contemporary World

Globalization, technology, conflicts, and social movements of the 21st century.

Islamic History

Beginnings of Islam, the life of the Prophet Muhammad, the Caliphates, Islamic empires, and contributions to science, culture, and law.
